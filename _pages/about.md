Hi there, thanks for visiting my space, this is xueyang! I'm a fifth-year CS PhD student at *Georgia Institute of Technology*, exploring the intersection of *design*, *wellbeing*, *social equity/justice*, and what it means to be human through digital systems.

Before starting my PhD, I worked at *SambaNova Systems* as a hardware engineer. I received Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from *University of Michigan* and *Shanghai Jiao Tong University*.
